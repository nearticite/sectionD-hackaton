Story

Beneath the fading town of Dirtmouth sleeps an ancient, ruined kingdom. Many are drawn below the surface, searching for riches, glory, or answers to old secrets. As the Hollow Knight, you must brave the depths, uncover the mysteries, and face the ancient evils that plague Hallownest.

Characters

Meet the key figures who shape your journey:

  • The Knight – A silent wanderer drawn to Hallownest.
  • Hornet – Protector of Hallownest, agile and fierce.
  • Quirrel – A curious traveler seeking knowledge.
